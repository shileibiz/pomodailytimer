About Us

Our Mission

Pomoday Timer was built to provide a distraction-free, private productivity tool that works entirely in your browser. We believe focused work shouldn't require signing up for yet another service, trusting your task data to a cloud provider, or fighting ads and notifications.

Why We Built This

Existing Pomodoro apps are either overloaded with features (project management, team collaboration, integrations) or require subscriptions for basic functionality. We wanted something that simply works: start a focus session, track your tasks, play some ambient sound, and get things done. No account, no data leaving your computer, no noise.

What Makes Pomoday Different

  • Truly private. Your tasks, stats, and settings live in your browser's local storage — never on a server.
  • Focus sounds included. White noise, rain, forest ambience, and lo-fi beats — all generated in your browser.
  • Dark mode. Easy on the eyes during late-night work sessions.
  • No paywalls. The full timer, task management, sounds, and statistics are free forever.

Who We Are

We are a small team of developers who believe productivity tools should respect your privacy. We build pure front-end applications — no servers, no databases, no data harvesting.
