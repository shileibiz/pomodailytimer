Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

1. Introduction

Pomoday Timer ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website pomodaytimer.pages.dev (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our free online Pomodoro timer.

2. No Data Collection

Pomoday Timer is a 100% client-side application. All timing, task management, statistics, and sound generation happen entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • Use cookies for tracking or analytics
  • Upload your tasks, stats, or preferences to any server
  • Require registration, login, or email address
  • Track your usage across sessions or devices

3. Local Storage

The tool uses your browser's localStorage to save your settings (theme preference, sound selection) and data (session statistics, task list) between visits. This data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

4. Third-Party Services

This website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare processes minimal, anonymized request data to deliver the website to you. We do not use Google Analytics or any other third-party analytics or tracking services.

5. Cookies

This website does not set any non-essential cookies. Cloudflare Pages may set strictly functional cookies required for CDN operation.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

Questions? Contact us at: privacy@pomodaytimer.pages.dev
